VisaBridge supports over 20 countries' visa processes including:
- United States (H1B, L1, O1, EB visas)
- Canada (Express Entry, Provincial Nominee Program)
- United Kingdom (Skilled Worker, Global Talent)
- Australia (Skilled Independent, Employer Nomination)
- Germany (Blue Card, Job Seeker Visa)
- Netherlands (Highly Skilled Migrant)
- Singapore (Employment Pass, Tech.Pass)
- New Zealand (Skilled Migrant Category)

Key features include:
- Document checklist generation
- Application timeline tracking
- Requirements verification
- Interview preparation
- Status monitoring
